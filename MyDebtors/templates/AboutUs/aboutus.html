Our Story


Notify is the first online “Debt Tracker for 
schools” app in West Africa. We first launched on
the 1st of January 2022 .

For 6 Months, we covered only 10 States in 
Nigeria . Then in July of 2022, we expanded and
now we are Nationwide. We have continued to
deliver excellent service to our 10,000 users and
counting, helping them track their debtors with
simplicity and transparency. 
                    
Notify is on a mission 'to give every school the
power to better track their debtors and manage
their finances', and we intend to be the best.
